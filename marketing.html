Starke Marketing

This was one of my first clients, Wolper, when I started working at Starke Marketing. I was tasked with finding Wolper's target audience, building a client persona, and coming up with a concept campaign. I created this as part of my concept campaign. "The new power suit." The one dress shirt for all occasions, fits for you but designed for your partner because when you look better, you perform better. The target audience was younger men, men who could afford a nice dress shirt but this wasn't just meant for work, it was for dates, cocktail parties, a weekend get-away. Older men always want to be younger, so target the youth and you will get the entire audience.
